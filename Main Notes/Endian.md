- **Big-Endian**: Big part (most significant byte) stored FIRST
- **Little-Endian**: Little part (least significant byte) stored FIRST

> The term is a reference to Gulliver's Travels where 2 factions went to war over which end an egg should be cracked from - the big or little end.
