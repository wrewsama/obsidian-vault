## The Matthew Effect
> For unto everyone that hath shall be give, and he shall have abundance, but from him that hath not shall be taken away even that which he hath.
> - Matthew 25:29
- early advantages in childhood lead to better opportunities to improve, compounding over time
- this is why the birthdays of pro athletes are heavily skewed toward the beginning of the year

## 10000 hours
- the magic number for reaching true expertise
- study on students in a top music school: among those with enough ability to get in to the school, the primary distinguisher is the amount of work put in
- even so, arbitrary advantages can be the difference between whether one even has a chance to put in that amount of work
