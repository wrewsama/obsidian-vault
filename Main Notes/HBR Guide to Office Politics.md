## Bosses
- first, try to see how the situation could actually be in your best interest
- improve your relationship by appealing to self interest
- find allies, especially among his peers

## Colleagues
- think about their real motive - they may not actually be adversarial
    - then address the root issues
- make alliances
- stand up to bullies, but be careful to not get pulled down to their level
- break into key "cliques" by exploiting any opportunities to work together, no matter how menial
