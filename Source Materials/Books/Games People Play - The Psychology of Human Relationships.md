## Analysis
- each individual has 3 ego states: Parent, Adult, and Child
- transactions occur between the ego states of each person (e.g. Adult-Adult, parent-child)
- ulterior transactions: 2 transactions between different ego states in 1 conversation, 1 at the social level and 1 at the psychological level
- Rituals: series of transactions, formed by social forces and intuitive computations about the context
- Game: series of complementary _ulterior transactions_ progressing toward a well-defined, predictable outcome
