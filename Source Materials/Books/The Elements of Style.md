## usage rules
- place a comma before `{and|or|but} <some independent clause>`
- do not join independent clauses with a comma
- A participle at the start of a sentence must refer to the subject (not the object)
    - participle: verb turned into an adjective (e.g. laugh (v)-> laughing (adj))
## composition rules
- 1 paragraph per topic
- for each paragraph, begin with a topic sentence, end with something related
    - e.g. re-state with extra emphasis, state some consequence
- use active voice (i.e. subject -> verb -> object, instead of object <- verb <- subject)
- put statements in positive form (e.g. he was late > he was not on time)
