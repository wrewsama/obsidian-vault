## Bosses
- first, try to see how the situation could actually be in your best interest
- improve your relationship by appealing to self interest
- find allies, especially among his peers

## Colleagues
- think about their real motive - they may not actually be adversarial
    - then address the root issues
- make alliances
- stand up to bullies, but be careful to not get pulled down to their level
- break into key "cliques" by exploiting any opportunities to work together, no matter how menial

## Surviving the Office Outing
- make a _reasonable_ effort to participate, if you're uncomfortable with the primary event, find something you can do to still show up
- give yourself a goal for the number of people you talk to
- use it to practice the skill of asking questions

## Skills
- conflict management
    - get comfortable with conflict
    - stay calm and use neutral, non-confrontational language
    - explain your goals and reasoning 
    - find out the other party's goals and reasoning, asking directly is an option
    - acknowledge the differences and work together to find a better plan
    - last resort: escalate to a superior
