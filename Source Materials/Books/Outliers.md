## The Matthew Effect
> For unto everyone that hath shall be give, and he shall have abundance, but from him that hath not shall be taken away even that which he hath.
> - Matthew 25:29
- early advantages in childhood lead to better opportunities to improve, compounding over time
- this is why the birthdays of pro athletes are heavily skewed toward the beginning of the year

## 10000 hours
- the magic number for reaching true expertise
- study on students in a top music school: among those with enough ability to get in to the school, the primary distinguisher is the amount of work put in
- even so, arbitrary advantages can be the difference between whether one even has a chance to put in that amount of work

## IQ
- IQ makes a difference, but it has diminishing returns
- above around 120, IQ differences translate to negligible differences in success

## practical intelligence
- definition
    - knowing what to say to whom
    - knowing when to say it
    - knowing how to say it for maximum effect
- also a sense of "entitlement" - to speak up when you need to
- often a product of one's upbringing

## opportunity
- for many outliers, they find a golden opportunity buried in setback
- many just happened to have worked extremely hard on a skill that became in-demand at just the right time
- hard work is a prison sentence only if it does not have meaning
    - those outliers found intrinsic value in the things they were doing
