## Intro
- **P/PC Balance**: when trying to maximise the Product (benefits), focus on Production Capability (ability to make those benefits)

## 1| Be Proactive
- your response must be a product of conscious choice, not a mere reaction to whatever the external conditions are
- take responsibility in making things happen
- Circle of influence: the subset of things we have control over
    - focus your efforts within that circle

## 2| Begin With The End in Mind
- write a personal mission statement
- visualise the goal

## 3| Put First Things First
- Classify your tasks with the Eisenhower Matrix
- Too much time on Urgent+Important => these crises will continue to consume more of your time
- Spend time on Import+Not Urgent => shrinks your Urgent+Important
    - use the goals in Habit 2 to discover these
- delegate
    - let's you focus on high-leverage activities
    - stewardship delegation: Let the people you delegate to own the task. Focus on the results, not the methods

## 4| Think Win-Win
- beware of thinking something is win-win when there's actually someone losing out
- having a "no deal" option makes this easier
- strategy
    - understand other party's key issues and concerns
    - determine their desired end result
    - find new options to achieve those

## 5| Seek to Understand, Then Be Understood
- analogy: diagnose the problem before prescribing a solution
- empathic listening: not just mimicking the other person, understand what the other person is feeling and reflect that
- very similar to labelling in [[Never Split the Difference]]

## 6| Synergise
- working together such that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts
- value the differences between (you and) other people
