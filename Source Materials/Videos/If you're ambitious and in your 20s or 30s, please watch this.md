- take more shots, you have nothing to lose and even if you do fail, you can experience
- don't follow your passion, follow what you're good at and your passion will follow
- focus on getting really good at one thing at a time
- work > network
- money loves speed: make the decision promptly as long as you know you can any resulting failure quickly, don't get stuck on making the "perfect" decision
- pay your ignorance debt: make the mistakes required to learn
- know the price of inaction
- get over yourself: Nobody cares what happened to you then, only what you can make happen now
- solve bigger problems: if you want to make a million dollars, you're gonna have to endure a million dollars worth of pain
- obsession is the price of entry
- work hard AND smart
- accept _consistent_ boredom, pain, and sacrifice

> In the beginning, you're in exploration. Later on, you get into exploitation. 
> - Naval Ravikant

> People who obsess about work-life balance are typically mediocre at both. 
> - Alex Hormozi

> It's not that we don't have enough time. It's that we waste the time that we have.
> - Seneca

## the core
- figure out what you want
- ignore the opinions of others
- do so much volume that it would be unreasonable to not be successful
