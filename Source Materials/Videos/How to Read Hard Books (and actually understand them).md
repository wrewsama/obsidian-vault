- pre-read background logic about the book
    - e.g. Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy
    - study guides
- stay actively engaged while reading by constantly asking questions about the text
- take concise notes
    - 3 points from each chapter / 3 points for the whole book
    - **Feynman Technique**: write summaries as if you were teaching to someone else
- **Loci Method**: use multisensory images in familiar locations to remember information
- read some difficult parts before bed, then sleep on it
- enjoy your reading
