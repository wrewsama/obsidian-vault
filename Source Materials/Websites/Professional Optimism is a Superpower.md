> **Performative Optimism**: _Hopium_. Cheap, blind faith that something will just work out somehow

> **Professional Optimism**: Accepting the problem, not giving up, and actively finding ways to solve it

- Do the homework to find out _what_ must be done to make the project work out
- Demonstrate a **bias for action**. Find out what must be done and do it, don't give up like pessimist and don't skirt around the problem like a performative optimist.
