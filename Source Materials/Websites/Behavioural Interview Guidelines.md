## non-verbal
- primary goal: make them like you
- be presentable
- speak clearly and fluently
- maintain relaxed and confident body language
- show excitement, initiative, desire to learn, and ability to handle ambiguity

## verbal
- tell me about yourself
    - 60-90s
    - where you came from, where you're going, why you're there
- why do you want to work in this company / industry
    - do prior research on the specific company for talking points
    - prepare good points about the industry
- use STAR format, prepare stories beforehand
- for "unfair" questions, like those asking for a weakness: answer honestly, then turn it into a positive light
- steer the interview by asking questions (e.g. would you like me to elaborate on XXX) or reframing their questions
- for Q&A, 2 questions, ask questions specific to the company (through research or from prior discussion)
